We request your business approval to perform the BRETT activity for the KSA Transfer Agency SA system, as part of the HSBC Data Backup and Recovery regulatory requirement.

Purpose of the Activity  
This exercise is required to simulate a data recovery scenario. In the event of any issue where both Production and Alternative sites are unavailable, the team must be able to restore production data in a testing environment to reproduce the issue and validate the fix. This activity ensures HSBC meets regulatory expectations for backup and recovery readiness.

Activity Overview  
- Take the backup from the Production environment.  
- Restore the backup in the UAT environment for recovery simulation.  
- Perform data masking to protect sensitive information.  
- Execute post-masking validation and cleanup to ensure no sensitive data remains exposed in the test environment.

This activity is planned to be performed after the Service Protection Period on 27 March.
